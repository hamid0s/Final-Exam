Final Exam

Summertime saga

Jenny

Jenny's Storyline will introduce you to your snobby roommate, and eventually form a relationship with them. To complete this path, you'll need a high Intelligence and Strength stat, a working computer, and a fair amount of money.

Mia

Mia is the girl next door to Erik who will talk to you at the beginning of school on Day 1 about studying together.

Roxxy

In order to complete Roxxy's storyline, you'll need to have a good Strength and Charisma stat, as well as some points in Dexterity. Visit the Stats page for more information on how to raise them.
